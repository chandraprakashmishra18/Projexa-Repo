Patek Philippe

Patek Philippe is a prestigious Swiss luxury watch company founded in 1839 in Geneva by Antoni Patek and Adrien Philippe. It became famous for inventing the keyless winding mechanism and for its many innovations in mechanical watchmaking. The company is family-owned by the Stern family since 1932 and remains independent. Patek Philippe watches are handcrafted with extreme precision and can take months or even years to produce.
